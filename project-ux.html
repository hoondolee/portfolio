EBIKE Redesign

UI/UXPrototypingFigma Design

Improved usability and interaction flow of an Ebike through research driven design of battery life and iterative prototyping to combat range anxiety.

The design focused on the persona of a day-tripper and had an autonomous function of Low Power Mode which was signaled with a vibration feedback in the handlebars. The app displays navigation calculations and optimized options to save battery in order to reach the final destination. There are also safety and luxury features like Bluetooth connection, emergency calling, and health/fitness links. One important feature is that our screen is greyscale and simply a display to avoid distractions while commuting.
